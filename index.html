About the Event

Humblefoolcup is a contest under Aparoksha 2016, annual Tech Fest of IIIT Allahabad conducted in the memory of Harsha SuryaNarayana A.K.A Humblefool. Harsha Suryanarayana, popularly known as humblefool to the coding community, and as Lord among his friends and the people close to him. He Died in an accident on the night of 15th June'14.This event is basically a coding competetion held at IIITA as a part of it's Tech-fest - Aparoksha.
Last year the event consisted of 2 rounds in which there was an active participation from students all over. This event had been a benchmark for Aparoksha'2k16. The winning team recieved a prize money of INR 40000.--------------------------------------------------Lorem Ipsum-----------------------------------------------
